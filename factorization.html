Prime Factorization

This is the prime factorization calculator. You just enter a number in the input area, click calculate, and then below you will see the prime factorization of that number.

The prime factorization of a number is all the prime factors that when you multiply together, you get the original number. Prime means that the number is only divisible by 1 and itself.

Examples of prime numbers are 2,3,5,7,11. For example, the prime factorization of 20=2*2*5.
